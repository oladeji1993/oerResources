Core-Values

The core values of Samuel Maduka University shall be: Collaboration, by aggressive and sustain linkages, partnerships, inter-and multi-disciplinarily to create new avenues of support, understanding and enquiry. Dedication, by commitments to uncompromising work ethic and critical issues of development of the State, the Nation and the world at large. Entrepreneurship, by openness to innovative ideas and skills, prudent use of resources and rapid response to challenges. Fairness, by commitments to staff and student welfare, personal development of the members of the University community and the immediate environments. Integrity, by paying attention to the highest ethical standards in all areas, commitments to being a University that welcomes talented minds from different backgrounds and challenges the to achieve optimum potentials Transparency, demonstrated by accountability, opening communication and pro-bity. Responsibility, by ensuring prudent and responsible stewardship of resources.
